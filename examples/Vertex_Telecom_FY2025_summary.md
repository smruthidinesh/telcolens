# Vertex Telecom — FY2025 Annual Summary

## Revenue & profitability
- Total revenue: **$22.4 billion**, up 5.1% year over year.
- Operating income: **$4.3 billion** (operating margin 19.2%).
- Free cash flow: **$5.6 billion**.

## Subscribers & churn
- Postpaid phone net additions: **1.28 million** for the full year.
- Blended postpaid churn: **0.91%**, improved from 1.04% in FY2024.
- Fixed wireless access subscribers: **6.4 million**, up from 4.9 million.

## ARPU & investment
- Postpaid ARPU: **$59.80**, up 2.4% year over year.
- Capital expenditures: **$3.1 billion**, focused on 5G standalone and fiber backhaul.
- Net debt to EBITDA: **2.5x**, down from 2.8x.

## Outlook
Management expects FY2026 service-revenue growth of 4–6%, with churn held below 1.0% and FWA
remaining the primary subscriber growth driver.
