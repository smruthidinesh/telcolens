Helix Mobile — Q4 2025 Earnings Summary

Helix Mobile reported total revenue of $5.84 billion in Q4 2025, up 6.7% year over year from
$5.47 billion in Q4 2024. Service revenue was $5.10 billion, led by postpaid wireless growth and
continued fixed wireless access (FWA) expansion.

Postpaid phone net additions were 401,000, the strongest quarter in three years. Total postpaid
churn improved to 0.84%, down from 0.99% in Q4 2024, helped by the new device-upgrade program and
an expanded loyalty tier. Prepaid churn was 2.6%, slightly better year over year.

Postpaid ARPU was $61.20, up 1.8% year over year. Fixed wireless access reached 2.7 million
subscribers, adding 305,000 in the quarter. Operating income was $1.12 billion with an operating
margin of 19.2%. Capital expenditures were $710 million, mostly for 5G standalone densification.
Free cash flow was $1.31 billion.

Management raised full-year guidance, citing record-low postpaid churn and accelerating FWA
adoption as the main drivers. Net debt to EBITDA was 2.4x, down from 2.7x a year ago.
